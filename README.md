
# 📊 Hardware Pulse | Sales Insights Dashboard

## Overview

Hardware Pulse is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze sales performance for a computer hardware company. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor revenue trends, evaluate market performance, identify high-value customers, and support data-driven business decisions.

The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow, including database querying, data cleaning, transformation, modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard development.

## Business Problem

The organization experienced a decline in sales performance across multiple regional markets. Decision-makers lacked a centralized reporting solution to monitor revenue trends and evaluate the factors contributing to the downturn.

Key business questions included:

* Which markets generate the highest and lowest revenue?
* Which customers contribute most to overall sales?
* Which products drive business performance?
* How have revenue trends changed over time?
* Where should sales recovery efforts be focused?



## Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this dashboard were to:

* Provide a centralized view of sales performance.
* Monitor revenue and sales quantity across regions.
* Identify top-performing customers and products.
* Analyze historical revenue trends.
* Enable stakeholders to make informed business decisions through interactive reporting.

## Data Source

The dashboard utilizes sales transaction data obtained from the company's ERP system.

### Dataset Includes

* Sales Transactions
* Customer Information
* Product Information
* Market Information
* Revenue and Sales Quantity Records


## Data Preparation and Transformation

Data preprocessing was performed using Power Query to ensure data quality and consistency.

### Data Cleaning Activities

* Removed null and invalid records.
* Standardized currency values across transactions.
* Converted USD transactions into INR using a predefined exchange rate.
* Eliminated duplicate and inconsistent entries.
* Filtered irrelevant market records.
* Validated revenue and sales quantity fields.

### Currency Normalization

A custom business rule was applied to convert foreign currency transactions into a unified reporting currency using an exchange rate of **95**.


## Data Model

The dashboard follows a relational data model connecting transactional and dimensional datasets.

### Fact Table

* Transactions

### Dimension Tables

* Customers
* Products
* Markets
* Date

Relationships were established to support accurate aggregation, filtering, and drill-down analysis.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following business metrics:

### Total Revenue

Measures overall sales revenue generated during the selected period.

### Total Sales Quantity

Measures the total volume of products sold.


## Dashboard Features

### Revenue Trend Analysis

Interactive line charts visualize revenue performance over time, helping identify growth patterns and sales declines.

### Market Performance Analysis

Bar charts compare revenue and sales quantity across markets, highlighting top-performing and underperforming regions.

### Customer Analysis

Identifies the highest revenue-generating customers and supports customer concentration analysis.

### Product Performance Analysis

Ranks products based on revenue contribution and sales volume.

### Interactive Filtering

Users can filter reports by:

* Year
* Month

This allows detailed exploration of specific reporting periods.


## Business Insights

### Revenue Concentration

Analysis revealed that a significant portion of revenue is generated by a limited number of customers and markets, indicating concentration risk.

### Regional Performance Monitoring

The dashboard enables rapid identification of underperforming markets, supporting targeted sales initiatives.

### Customer Prioritization

High-value customers can be monitored closely to strengthen retention and account management strategies.

### Inventory Planning Support

Sales quantity analysis helps align inventory allocation with regional demand patterns.
